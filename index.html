Introducing:

Swatches

BerilliumCSS introduces an innovative feature known as "Swatches." This functionality allows developers to effortlessly implement a cohesive color scheme throughout their web projects. Swatches in BerilliumCSS are pre-defined color sets that can be applied to various elements, ensuring consistency and harmony in design. These swatches are customizable, offering a range of hues and shades to fit the specific aesthetic needs of any website. With this feature, BerilliumCSS simplifies the process of color management, making it more accessible for developers to maintain a professional and visually appealing color palette across their web applications.
